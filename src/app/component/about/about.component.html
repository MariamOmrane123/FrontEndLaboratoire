Welcome to LabAPI

The laboratory on Development and Control of Distributed Applications (LabAPI) was created in October 2005 at the Department of Computer Engineering and Applied Mathematics of the National School of Engineers of Sfax. The main activities carried on bythe members of this laboratory include:

  • Scientific research
  • Training and advising of young researchers
  • Participation in the socio-economic life
  • Contribution to national and international research projects
  • Organization of national and international scientific conferences and workshops

LabAPI is composed by 100 researchers
